About our HOA

Step back in time as you enter the gates of one of Valdosta’s most sought-after communities, Grove Pointe. The character and detail of this high-end residential community focuses on classic traditional architecture. Set on a breathtaking site, Grove Pointe offers beautifully preserved Spanish Moss draped Live Oaks. These large estate homes sites are the perfect place to build your dream home and to find peace and quiet with complete privacy.
